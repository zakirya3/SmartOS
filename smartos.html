Our Mission

Built for
Pakistan's
Future

Eliminating structural dependency on foreign software — delivering a self-reliant, sustainable operating platform built entirely in Pakistan, for Pakistan.

SmartOS is custom-engineered to empower Pakistani schools with a secure, high-performance, and completely localized operating infrastructure. By deploying native open-source frameworks, our foundational goal is to completely eliminate expensive software licensing fees and reduce structural dependency on monolithic foreign corporate software like Microsoft Windows. We believe that national educational infrastructure should be self-reliant, sustainable, and built locally.
